Hello, I'm Owen

My academic journey has been centered around the intersection of Marine Science and GIS, where I've actively participated in various student projects and gained practical experience through work or internships.

This website serves as a professional platform to showcase my work. I hope the content here effectively introduces myself and highlights my capabilities.
